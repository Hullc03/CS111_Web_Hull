Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions. AI is a broad field that includes machine learning, natural language processing, and robotics. It enables computers to learn from data, recognize patterns, and make decisions with minimal human intervention.

Applications of AI

AI has numerous applications across various industries, including healthcare, finance, and transportation. In healthcare, AI is used for diagnostics, personalized medicine, and robotic surgery. In finance, it helps with fraud detection and algorithmic trading. Self-driving cars are a well-known example of AI in the transportation sector. As AI continues to advance, its potential applications grow exponentially.
